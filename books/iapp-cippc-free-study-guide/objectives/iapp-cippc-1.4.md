# 1.4: Understand the CSA Model Code for the Protection of Personal Information

Source: IAPP CIPP/C Free Study Guide

---

**Note**: This source provides only a brief mention of the CSA Model Code as a key
subject matter area. For comprehensive exam preparation on this objective, refer
to the official CIPP/C textbook "Canadian Privacy: Data Protection Law and Policy
for the Practitioner."

## Key concepts

- **CSA Model Code**: Listed as one of the Canadian model codes for privacy
  compliance that CIPP/C candidates must understand
- **GAPP framework**: Mentioned alongside CSA as another model code framework
  relevant to Canadian privacy compliance

## Source coverage

This free study guide identifies the CSA Model Code as exam-relevant content but
does not provide substantive detail. The body of knowledge outline places this
objective under:

- **Domain I**: Introduction to Privacy in Canada (28% weight)
- **Objective 1.4**: Understand the CSA Model Code for the Protection of Personal
  Information

## Related topics

- PIPEDA: The CSA Model Code's 10 principles are incorporated as Schedule 1 of
  PIPEDA
- Provincial private-sector laws: Alberta PIPA, BC PIPA, and Quebec's Act
  Respecting the Protection of Personal Information in the Private Sector build
  on similar principles
- Privacy commissioners: Oversight bodies enforce compliance with these
  principles

## Exam preparation note

The study guide recommends 30 hours of preparation and emphasizes studying the
official textbook for detailed coverage of topics like the CSA Model Code.
Candidates should ensure they understand:

- The 10 CSA principles and their application
- How the CSA Model Code influenced Canadian privacy legislation
- The relationship between model codes and enforceable law
