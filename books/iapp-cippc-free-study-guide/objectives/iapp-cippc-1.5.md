# 1.5: Identify differences between privacy and data protection frameworks

Source: CIPP/C Free Study Guide (IAPP, 2025)

## Key concepts

- **Canadian privacy model codes**: The CSA Model Code for the Protection of
  Personal Information and Generally Accepted Privacy Principles (GAPP)
  provide standards for privacy compliance in the private sector.
  - CSA Model Code: Canadian standards framework; foundational to PIPEDA
  - GAPP: Professional framework with measurable criteria

- **Framework purpose**: Privacy model codes inform Canadian legislation and
  provide reference standards for understanding foundational privacy principles.

## Related topics

- **CSA Model Code**: Canadian adaptation of OECD principles; became Schedule 1
  of PIPEDA
- **GAPP**: Professional governance framework for privacy compliance
- **PIPEDA**: Federal private sector law incorporating CSA principles
- **Provincial private sector laws**: Alberta PIPA, BC PIPA, Quebec's Act
  Respecting the Protection of Personal Information in the Private Sector

## Scope limitations
